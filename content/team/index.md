# SESS Leadership Team

Our executive team consists of passionate engineering students dedicated to building an amazing community.

## Executive Positions

The SESS executive team includes:

- **President** - Overall leadership and vision
- **Vice President (Internal)** - Internal events and member engagement
- **Vice President (External)** - Connections with ESSCO, CFES, and industry
- **Treasurer** - Finances and budgeting
- **Secretary** - Communications and documentation
- **Event Coordinators** - Planning and executing events
- **Social Media Manager** - Online presence and announcements

## Committee Members

Beyond the executive, we have committees focused on:

- **Events & Activities** - Organizing engaging events throughout the year
- **Technical Workshops** - Planning coding, design, and technical learning sessions
- **Competitions** - Supporting Seneca teams in competitions
- **Mentorship** - Connecting students with mentors and professionals
- **Marketing & Outreach** - Promoting SESS and engaging the community

## Want to Join the Team?

We're always looking for passionate student leaders! Opportunities to get involved include:

- ✅ Volunteering for events
- 🙋 Running for an executive position (elections typically in spring)
- 🎯 Joining a committee
- 💡 Proposing new initiatives

**Interested?** [Contact us](/contact) or talk to any exec member at an event!

# Join the Seneca Engineering Students' Society

## Why Join SESS?
✨ **Network** - Connect with peers, mentors, and industry professionals  
🚀 **Grow** - Develop technical and leadership skills  
🏆 **Compete** - Participate in local, provincial, and national competitions  
🎓 **Learn** - Attend workshops and gain real-world experience  
💼 **Advance** - Build your professional network for your engineering career  

## Become an Official Member

Ready to make it official? Join SESS through the Seneca Students' Federation portal:

### 👉 [Join SESS on the SSF Portal](https://clubs.ssfinc.ca/sess/home/)

Joining officially through the SSF gives you full access to all member benefits, voting rights, and eligibility for funded events and competitions.

---

## Getting Involved

Joining SESS is simple—there's no formal membership process to attend events! Here's how to get started:

### 1. **Attend an Event**
Show up to any SESS event or activity. It doesn't matter if it's your first day or your last semester—everyone is welcome!

### 2. **Join Our Discord**
Connect with the community online. Our Discord is where we share event announcements, collaborate on projects, and hang out.

📱 [Join our Discord](https://discord.gg/KVvrrJ7J)

### 3. **Follow Our Social Media**
Stay updated on upcoming events and news.

- 📸 Instagram: [@seneca.engsoc](https://www.instagram.com/seneca.engsoc?igsh=MTA2dmczbjZ0N3MzOQ==)
- 💼 LinkedIn: [Seneca Engineering Students' Society](https://www.linkedin.com/company/seneca-engineering-students-society/posts/?feedView=all)
- 🐙 GitHub: [Seneca-Engineering-Students-Society](https://github.com/Seneca-Engineering-Students-Society)

### 4. **Consider Leadership**
Interested in taking on a bigger role? We're always looking for people to help organize events, lead committees, or take on executive positions. Talk to any exec member at an event, or [contact us](/contact).

## For First-Year Students
New to engineering at Seneca? SESS is the perfect place to:
- Meet people in your program
- Learn about campus resources and opportunities
- Get advice from upper-year students
- Start exploring your engineering interests

**Don't miss our Frosh Event** - happening in September!

## Beyond SESS
As a member, you'll also have access to opportunities with:
- **ESSCO** - Provincial events connecting Ontario engineering schools
- **CFES** - National competitions and conferences across Canada
- **Industry Partners** - Networking and potential internship opportunities

## Questions?
Ready to get involved or have questions about SESS? **[Contact us](/contact)** and we'll be happy to help!
